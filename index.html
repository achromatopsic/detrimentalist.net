My Philosophy

There's one thing you never get told when you're young: You never grow up. Your personhood is an everchanging, continuous, transient state of existence. To "grow up" in the most normative sense is to surrender the things that bring you joy. Don't do that. Be yourself, be continued.

  • Existing is the highest form of performance art.

  • Datamining is the ultimate form of love. To be loved is to be known.

  • Imitation is the sincerest form of flattery.

  • No matter where you are, everyone is always connected.

  • The Worm loves you!
